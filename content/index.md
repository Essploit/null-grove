🌱 Welcome to The Null Grove

Hello, wanderer. I go by **Essploit** — and welcome to **The Null Grove**, my personal digital garden.

This is a living archive of my study notes, experiments, and deep dives into the world of **Cybersecurity**. It’s where I document what I learn, break things, analyze the pieces, and rebuild with a clearer understanding.

In this space, you’ll find a mix of structured notes, raw thoughts, and cryptic breadcrumbs — all part of my journey through the ever-shifting digital underworld.

### My interests include:

- **Offensive Security & Pentesting** – Breaking into systems to understand how to defend them.
    
- **Secure Development** – Writing code with a hacker’s mindset: clean, efficient, and hardened.
    
- **System Engineering** – Architecting digital infrastructure that balances performance with paranoia.
    
- **Cyber Threat Intelligence** – Tracking actors, analyzing malware, and studying digital warfare.
    
- **Reverse Engineering** – Pulling apart binaries, malware, and obfuscated logic.
This garden isn’t polished — it’s meant to grow wild. Some ideas are seeds, others are in full bloom. Everything here is in flux, just like the threats out there.

Explore. Poke around. Get lost in the noise.
